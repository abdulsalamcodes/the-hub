About

This is an educational platform where members memorize some specific lines of poem on weekly basis. The poem at hand is Qawaaid-Fiqeeyyat of Al-Imam Abdulrahman As-sa'ad. May Allah be pleased with him

The activities is coordinated by Uztaaz Abu Ukaasha Yusuf Abdulrahman Ajamentala. May All preserve him upon goodness. The main goal is to foster learning and make it easy for all. It is also about spreading the Sunnah as Al-Imaam Ibn Uthaymeen as also encouraged.

Whenever you have the opportunity to spread the sunnah, then do so. You will have the reward of the sunnah and the reward of ones who act upon it till the Day of Judgement.
Sharh Riyadh As-saliheen by Shaykh Muhammad ibn Uthaymeen

Membership

Anyone can participate in this beneficial hub by contacting the coordinator via his watsApp number 09064777159

How it work

5 lines of poem is memorized on weekly basis. The coordinator do send the voice note of the portion to take to the watsApp group page

The explanation of the poem is also sent with it. Every member is then charge to memorize the portion before the week ends.

Latest on Sunday, all participating menber must forward a voice note of the portion of the poem taken that week and the grading will be done based on the voice note sent.
